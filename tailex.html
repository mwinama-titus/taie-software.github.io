About TaiLex

TaiLex is the reader version of the Tai-E legal authorities platform. It gives users access to selected legal excerpts and related case information through a clear Windows desktop interface.

The application is intended to support legal study, research and professional reference by making it easier to browse excerpts and copy properly presented case citations.

Legal Research Support

Use curated excerpts as a starting point for deeper research and authority checking.

Windows Desktop Interface

Frequently Asked Questions

What is TaiLex?

TaiLex is a legal authorities reader that provides access to selected case excerpts, citation information and related legal material.

Can I add or edit excerpts in the reader version?

The reader version is primarily intended for browsing and using published legal content. Database management and owner controls are separate from the reader experience.

Can I copy citations?

Yes. TaiLex is designed to allow users to copy available legal citations for research and written work.

Should I verify the original authority?

Yes. Important quotations, citations and propositions of law should always be checked against the original judgment, legislation or official legal source.

Where can I download TaiLex?
