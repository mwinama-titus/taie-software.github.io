About Tai-E OCR

Tai-E OCR helps users obtain text from scanned documents, PDF pages and images. It provides a desktop-based workflow for turning image-based content into readable text that can be reviewed, copied, edited or used in other applications.

Image-to-Text Recognition

Extract text from supported image files containing printed or scanned text.

Scanned PDF Processing

Editable Text Output

Review and work with recognised text instead of manually typing the entire document.

Document Productivity

Reduce the time required to reproduce text from scanned records and document images.

Simple Desktop Interface

Local Processing

Frequently Asked Questions

What does OCR mean?

OCR means Optical Character Recognition. It is the process of identifying text inside an image or scanned document and converting it into usable digital text.

Can Tai-E OCR process scanned documents?

Yes. Tai-E OCR is intended to recognise text contained in scanned documents, PDF pages and image files.

Can OCR results contain mistakes?

Yes. OCR accuracy can be affected by image quality, document layout, font style, scanning quality and the clarity of the original text. Recognised text should be reviewed before important use.
