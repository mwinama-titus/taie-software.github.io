About Ti2Pix

Ti2Pix is designed for users who need a simple way to convert documents and images without uploading files to an online service. It processes files locally on the computer, helping preserve privacy while providing fast and reliable output.

PDF Conversion

Convert PDF pages into high-quality images suitable for sharing, printing, editing or archiving.

TIFF Conversion

Process single-page and multi-page TIFF files through a straightforward desktop interface.

Image Conversion

Convert common image formats while maintaining useful output quality.

Batch Processing

Convert multiple files in one operation instead of processing every file separately.

Completely Offline

No internet connection is required for normal file conversion.

Privacy Focused

Files remain on the user's computer during the conversion process.
